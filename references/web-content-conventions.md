# Web Content Conventions

Guidelines for generating web pages, landing pages, and product pages.

---

## Content Types

### Landing Pages
**Purpose:** Convert visitors into leads or customers
**Length:** 1,000-2,500 words
**Structure:** Hero → Problem → Solution → Features → Social Proof → CTA

### Web Pages
**Purpose:** Inform and guide visitors
**Length:** 500-1,500 words
**Structure:** Header → Main Content → Supporting Sections → CTA

### Product Pages
**Purpose:** Sell specific products
**Length:** 800-2,000 words
**Structure:** Product Hero → Features → Benefits → Specs → Reviews → CTA

---

## Landing Page Structure

### Hero Section
- Compelling headline (6-12 words)
- Subheadline explaining value proposition
- Primary CTA button (above the fold)
- Hero image or video
- Trust indicators (logos, badges)

### Problem Section
- Identify the pain point clearly
- Make it relatable and specific
- Use emotional language
- 2-3 paragraphs maximum

### Solution Section
- Present your offer as the answer
- Focus on transformation, not features
- Use "before and after" language
- Include visual representation

### Features Section
- 3-6 key features
- Icon + headline + description format
- Focus on benefits, not just features
- Keep descriptions to 2-3 sentences

### Social Proof Section
- Customer testimonials (3-5)
- Case study highlights
- Trust badges and certifications
- Client logos
- Statistics and results

### CTA Section
- Clear, action-oriented headline
- Remove friction (free trial, no credit card, etc.)
- Multiple CTA buttons throughout page
- Final strong CTA at bottom

---

## Web Page Structure

### Header
- Clear page title
- Brief introduction (1-2 paragraphs)
- Optional breadcrumb navigation
- Table of contents for long pages

### Main Content
- Organized with H2 and H3 subheadings
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points for lists
- Images and visuals every 300-500 words
- Internal links to related pages

### Supporting Sections
- FAQ section (if applicable)
- Related resources
- Next steps
- Contact information

### Footer CTA
- Summarize key takeaway
- Clear next action
- Link to relevant pages

---

## Product Page Structure

### Product Hero
- Product name and tagline
- High-quality product images (multiple angles)
- Price and availability
- Primary CTA (Add to Cart, Buy Now)
- Key benefit statement

### Features & Benefits
- Feature list with icons
- Each feature linked to specific benefit
- Use "you" language (customer-focused)
- Highlight unique selling points

### Product Description
- Detailed explanation of what it is
- Who it's for
- How it works
- What's included
- Use cases and applications

### Technical Specifications
- Organized table format
- Dimensions, materials, compatibility
- Technical details
- Warranty and support information

### Customer Reviews
- Star rating and review count
- Featured reviews (3-5)
- Review highlights
- Verified purchase badges

### Related Products
- Cross-sell opportunities
- "Customers also bought"
- Product bundles
- Accessories

---

## Best Practices

### Headlines
- Use power words (proven, guaranteed, exclusive)
- Include numbers when possible
- Address pain points or desires
- Keep under 70 characters for SEO

### Copy Principles
- Write in second person ("you")
- Use active voice
- Short sentences and paragraphs
- Scannable formatting
- Conversational tone

### Visual Hierarchy
- Most important information first
- Use whitespace effectively
- Contrast for CTAs
- Consistent styling
- Mobile-responsive design

### CTAs
- Action-oriented verbs (Get, Start, Join, Download)
- Create urgency (Limited time, Only X left)
- Remove friction (Free, No commitment)
- Use contrasting colors
- Multiple CTAs throughout page

### Trust Building
- Customer testimonials
- Security badges
- Money-back guarantees
- Industry certifications
- Press mentions
- Customer count/social proof

## SEO Optimization

### On-Page Elements
- Title tag (50-60 characters)
- Meta description (150-160 characters)
- H1 tag (one per page, includes keyword)
- H2-H6 subheadings (structured hierarchy)
- Image alt text
- URL slug (short, keyword-rich)

### Content Optimization
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Secondary keywords naturally throughout
- Internal links to related pages
- External links to authoritative sources
- Schema markup for products
- FAQ schema for Q&A sections

### Performance
- Fast loading time (under 3 seconds)
- Mobile-optimized
- Compressed images
- Minimal redirects
- Clean code

---

## Conversion Optimization

### Above the Fold
- Clear value proposition
- Primary CTA visible
- Trust indicators
- No distractions

### Friction Reduction
- Minimal form fields
- Guest checkout option
- Clear pricing
- No hidden costs
- Easy navigation

### Urgency & Scarcity
- Limited time offers
- Stock indicators
- Countdown timers
- Exclusive deals
- Social proof (X people viewing)

### Risk Reversal
- Money-back guarantee
- Free trial period
- No credit card required
- Cancel anytime
- Satisfaction guarantee

## Content Length Guidelines

**Landing Pages:**
- Short form: 1,000-1,500 words (simple offer)
- Long form: 2,000-2,500 words (complex offer, high-ticket)

**Web Pages:**
- About pages: 500-800 words
- Service pages: 800-1,200 words
- Resource pages: 1,000-1,500 words

**Product Pages:**
- Simple products: 800-1,200 words
- Complex products: 1,500-2,000 words
- High-ticket products: 2,000+ words

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

**Content Mistakes:**
- Too much jargon or technical language
- Feature-focused instead of benefit-focused
- Weak or unclear value proposition
- No clear call-to-action
- Too much text without visuals
- Generic, non-specific copy

**Design Mistakes:**
- Cluttered layout
- Poor mobile experience
- Slow loading time
- Hard to find CTA
- Inconsistent styling
- Too many distractions

**SEO Mistakes:**
- Missing title tags or meta descriptions
- Duplicate content
- No header structure
- Missing alt text
- Broken links
- Not mobile-friendly

## Examples

### Landing Page Example (SaaS)

**Hero:**
```
Headline: "Close More Deals in Half the Time"
Subheadline: "Sales automation software that helps B2B teams close 3x more deals without hiring more reps"
CTA: "Start Free Trial"
```

**Problem:**
```
"Tired of losing deals because your sales team is buried in admin work? 
You're not alone. The average sales rep spends only 34% of their time 
actually selling. The rest? Endless data entry, follow-ups, and reporting."
```

**Solution:**
```
"SalesFlow automates the busy work so your team can focus on what they 
do best: building relationships and closing deals. Our AI-powered platform 
handles follow-ups, updates your CRM, and even schedules meetings."
```

### Product Page Example (E-commerce)

**Product Hero:**
```
Product: "Premium Wireless Headphones"
Tagline: "Studio-Quality Sound, Anywhere"
Price: "$299.99"
CTA: "Add to Cart"
Key Benefit: "40-hour battery life with active noise cancellation"
```

**Features:**
```
- Active Noise Cancellation: Block out distractions and immerse yourself in pure sound
- 40-Hour Battery: Listen all week on a single charge
- Premium Materials: Soft leather ear cups and aluminum frame
- Multipoint Connection: Connect to two devices simultaneously
```
