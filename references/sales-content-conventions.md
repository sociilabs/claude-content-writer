# Sales Content Conventions

Guidelines for generating sales funnels, case studies, testimonials, and product descriptions.

---

## Content Types

### Sales Funnels
**Purpose:** Guide prospects through buying journey
**Components:** Landing page → Lead magnet → Email sequence → Sales page → Checkout
**Length:** Varies by stage
**Structure:** Awareness → Interest → Decision → Action

### Case Studies
**Purpose:** Demonstrate real-world results and build credibility
**Length:** 1,000-2,000 words
**Structure:** Challenge → Solution → Results → Testimonial

### Testimonials
**Purpose:** Provide social proof and build trust
**Length:** 50-300 words
**Structure:** Problem → Experience → Result → Recommendation

### Product Descriptions
**Purpose:** Inform and persuade potential buyers
**Length:** 150-500 words
**Structure:** Hook → Features → Benefits → Specifications → CTA

---

## Sales Funnel Structure

### Stage 1: Awareness (Top of Funnel)
**Goal:** Attract and educate prospects
**Content Types:**
- Blog articles
- Social media posts
- Educational videos
- Infographics
- Free tools

**Messaging:**
- Problem-focused
- Educational tone
- No hard sell
- Build awareness
- Establish authority

### Stage 2: Interest (Middle of Funnel)
**Goal:** Engage and nurture leads
**Content Types:**
- Lead magnets (ebooks, guides, templates)
- Webinars
- Email courses
- Comparison guides
- Case studies

**Messaging:**
- Solution-focused
- Demonstrate expertise
- Address objections
- Build trust
- Soft product mentions

### Stage 3: Decision (Bottom of Funnel)
**Goal:** Convert leads to customers
**Content Types:**
- Product demos
- Free trials
- Consultation offers
- Detailed product pages
- Pricing pages

**Messaging:**
- Product-focused
- Clear value proposition
- Strong social proof
- Risk reversal
- Urgency and scarcity

### Stage 4: Action (Conversion)
**Goal:** Close the sale
**Content Types:**
- Sales pages
- Checkout pages
- Order forms
- Upsell offers
- Thank you pages

**Messaging:**
- Benefit-driven
- Remove friction
- Clear CTAs
- Trust signals
- Guarantee prominent

## Case Study Structure

### Title
- Include client name (if allowed)
- Highlight key result
- Use numbers when possible
- Format: "How [Client] Achieved [Result] with [Solution]"

### Executive Summary
- 2-3 sentences
- Key challenge, solution, and result
- Compelling hook
- Include main metric

### Challenge Section
**Length:** 200-300 words
**Content:**
- Client background
- Specific problem faced
- Impact of problem
- Why it mattered
- Previous attempts to solve

**Format:**
- Start with context
- Describe pain points
- Quantify impact
- Set the stage

### Solution Section
**Length:** 300-500 words
**Content:**
- Your approach
- Why you were chosen
- Implementation process
- Timeline
- Key features used

**Format:**
- Explain methodology
- Show your expertise
- Highlight unique approach
- Include specifics

### Results Section
**Length:** 200-400 words
**Content:**
- Quantifiable outcomes
- Before and after metrics
- ROI calculations
- Unexpected benefits
- Long-term impact

**Format:**
- Lead with biggest win
- Use specific numbers
- Show multiple metrics
- Include timeframe

### Testimonial Quote
- Direct quote from client
- Authentic and specific
- Highlights key benefit
- Includes name and title
- Photo if possible

### Call-to-Action
- Relevant next step
- Similar use case offer
- Consultation or demo
- Related resources

## Testimonial Structure

### Short Testimonial (50-100 words)
**Format:**
```
"[Specific result or benefit]. [What they liked most]. [Recommendation]."
- [Name], [Title] at [Company]
```

**Example:**
```
"We increased our conversion rate by 47% in just 3 months. The team was responsive and the platform was intuitive. I'd recommend this to any B2B company serious about growth."
- Sarah Johnson, VP Marketing at TechCorp
```

### Medium Testimonial (100-200 words)
**Structure:**
- Problem statement
- Why they chose you
- Experience working together
- Specific results
- Overall recommendation

### Long Testimonial (200-300 words)
**Structure:**
- Detailed problem description
- Selection process
- Implementation experience
- Multiple results/benefits
- Unexpected wins
- Strong recommendation
- Future plans

### Video Testimonial Script
**Length:** 60-90 seconds
**Structure:**
1. Introduction (5 seconds)
2. Problem (15 seconds)
3. Solution (20 seconds)
4. Results (30 seconds)
5. Recommendation (20 seconds)

## Product Description Structure

### Hook (First 50 words)
- Grab attention immediately
- Lead with main benefit
- Create desire
- Use power words
- Address pain point

### Features Section
**Format:** Bullet points
**Content:**
- 5-8 key features
- Specific and concrete
- Technical details
- What it includes
- Unique capabilities

**Formula:** [Feature] + [Benefit]
Example: "Cloud-based storage - Access your files from anywhere"

### Benefits Section
**Format:** Short paragraphs
**Content:**
- How it improves life
- Problems it solves
- Time/money saved
- Emotional benefits
- Transformation promised

### Use Cases
- Who it's for
- Common scenarios
- Industry applications
- Problem-solution pairs
- Real-world examples

### Specifications
**Format:** Table or list
**Content:**
- Dimensions/size
- Materials
- Technical specs
- Compatibility
- Requirements

### Social Proof
- Customer ratings
- Review count
- Awards/certifications
- Media mentions
- Usage statistics

### Guarantee/Warranty
- Money-back guarantee
- Warranty period
- Return policy
- Risk reversal
- Support included

### Call-to-Action
- Clear next step
- Benefit-focused button
- Urgency element
- Multiple CTAs
- Add to cart prominent

## Sales Copy Principles

### Copywriting Formulas

**AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)**
- Grab attention with headline
- Build interest with benefits
- Create desire with proof
- Drive action with CTA

**PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)**
- Identify the problem
- Agitate the pain
- Present your solution

**FAB (Features-Advantages-Benefits)**
- List the feature
- Explain the advantage
- Describe the benefit

**4 Ps (Picture-Promise-Prove-Push)**
- Paint a picture
- Make a promise
- Prove it works
- Push to action

### Persuasion Techniques

**Social Proof**
- Customer testimonials
- Case studies
- User counts
- Expert endorsements
- Media coverage

**Scarcity**
- Limited quantity
- Time-limited offers
- Exclusive access
- Seasonal availability
- One-time opportunity

**Urgency**
- Countdown timers
- Limited spots
- Price increases
- Bonuses expiring
- Act now messaging

**Authority**
- Expert credentials
- Industry awards
- Certifications
- Years in business
- Notable clients

**Reciprocity**
- Free value first
- Lead magnets
- Free trials
- Bonus content
- Helpful resources

## Sales Page Structure

### Above the Fold
- Compelling headline
- Subheadline with benefit
- Hero image/video
- Primary CTA button
- Trust indicators

### Problem Section
- Identify pain points
- Make it relatable
- Use emotional language
- Show understanding
- Build urgency

### Solution Section
- Introduce your offer
- Explain how it works
- Highlight key benefits
- Show transformation
- Use visuals

### Features & Benefits
- Detailed feature list
- Each with clear benefit
- Icons or images
- Scannable format
- Prioritize by importance

### Social Proof
- Customer testimonials (3-5)
- Case study highlights
- Video testimonials
- Trust badges
- Media logos

### Pricing Section
- Clear pricing tiers
- Value comparison
- Most popular highlighted
- Money-back guarantee
- Payment options

### FAQ Section
- Address objections
- Remove doubts
- Build confidence
- Provide details
- Link to support

### Final CTA
- Recap key benefits
- Create urgency
- Remove friction
- Strong button copy
- Multiple payment options

## Objection Handling

### Common Objections

**"It's too expensive"**
- Show ROI
- Break down cost
- Compare alternatives
- Highlight value
- Offer payment plans

**"I need to think about it"**
- Create urgency
- Limited-time bonus
- Address real concern
- Provide more info
- Follow-up sequence

**"I'm not sure it will work for me"**
- Case studies
- Testimonials
- Free trial
- Money-back guarantee
- Specific use cases

**"I don't have time"**
- Show time savings
- Easy implementation
- Done-for-you options
- Quick wins
- Support available

**"I need approval"**
- ROI calculator
- Presentation materials
- Case studies
- Trial period
- Stakeholder resources

## Conversion Optimization

### Headline Testing
- Benefit-focused vs. feature-focused
- Question vs. statement
- Specific numbers vs. general
- Short vs. long
- Emotional vs. logical

### CTA Optimization
- Button text variations
- Color testing
- Size and placement
- Number of CTAs
- Above/below fold

### Social Proof Placement
- Above the fold
- Near pricing
- Throughout page
- Video vs. text
- Quantity vs. quality

### Pricing Presentation
- Single vs. multiple tiers
- Annual vs. monthly
- Anchor pricing
- Decoy pricing
- Value stacking

## Sales Funnel Examples

### SaaS Sales Funnel

**Stage 1: Blog Post (Awareness)**
- Topic: "10 Ways to Improve Team Productivity"
- CTA: Download free productivity template

**Stage 2: Lead Magnet (Interest)**
- Free template with email capture
- Welcome email with tips
- 5-day email course

**Stage 3: Webinar (Decision)**
- "How to 10x Your Team's Output"
- Product demo included
- Special offer for attendees

**Stage 4: Trial (Action)**
- 14-day free trial
- Onboarding sequence
- Upgrade prompts
- Success stories

### E-commerce Sales Funnel

**Stage 1: Social Media (Awareness)**
- Product showcase posts
- Educational content
- User-generated content
- CTA: Visit website

**Stage 2: Product Page (Interest)**
- Detailed product info
- Customer reviews
- Size guide
- CTA: Add to cart

**Stage 3: Cart (Decision)**
- Product summary
- Shipping options
- Discount codes
- Trust badges

**Stage 4: Checkout (Action)**
- Streamlined form
- Multiple payment options
- Order summary
- Upsell offers
