# Email Content Conventions

Guidelines for generating newsletters, email campaigns, and email sequences.

---

## Content Types

### Newsletters
**Purpose:** Regular updates, content sharing, relationship building
**Length:** 300-800 words
**Frequency:** Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly
**Structure:** Header → Main Content → CTA → Footer

### Email Campaigns
**Purpose:** Promote specific offer, product, or event
**Length:** 200-500 words
**Frequency:** One-time or limited series
**Structure:** Subject Line → Preview Text → Body → CTA

### Email Sequences
**Purpose:** Automated nurture, onboarding, or sales funnel
**Length:** 150-400 words per email
**Frequency:** Triggered or time-based series (3-10 emails)
**Structure:** Welcome → Education → Value → Offer → Close

---

## Newsletter Structure

### Subject Line
- 40-50 characters optimal
- Clear value proposition
- Avoid spam triggers
- Personalization when possible
- A/B test different approaches

### Preview Text
- 85-100 characters
- Complements subject line
- Provides additional context
- Entices to open

### Header
- Brand logo
- Navigation (optional)
- Personalized greeting
- Issue number/date

### Main Content
- Lead story or featured content
- 2-4 supporting sections
- Mix of content types (articles, tips, news)
- Scannable formatting
- Images and visuals

### CTA Section
- Clear primary action
- Secondary CTAs (optional)
- Button or prominent link
- Benefit-focused copy

### Footer
- Social media links
- Contact information
- Unsubscribe link (required)
- Physical address (required for commercial)
- Preference center link

---

## Email Campaign Structure

### Subject Line Formula
- Benefit + Urgency: "Save 30% Today Only"
- Curiosity + Value: "The Secret to [Desired Outcome]"
- Question + Solution: "Struggling with X? Try This"
- Personalization + Offer: "[Name], Your Exclusive Invite"

### Opening
- Hook in first sentence
- Address pain point or desire
- Establish relevance
- Personal tone

### Body
- One main message
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points for features/benefits
- Social proof (testimonials, stats)
- Urgency or scarcity

### CTA
- Single, clear action
- Action-oriented button text
- Repeated 2-3 times
- Remove friction
- Create urgency

### P.S. Section
- Reinforce main benefit
- Add urgency
- Alternative CTA
- Personal note

## Email Sequence Structure

### Welcome Email (Email 1)
**Timing:** Immediate
**Goal:** Set expectations, deliver promised content
**Content:**
- Thank you for subscribing
- What to expect
- Deliver lead magnet
- Set next email expectation
- Soft CTA

### Education Email (Emails 2-4)
**Timing:** Days 2-7
**Goal:** Build trust, provide value
**Content:**
- Helpful tips or insights
- Case studies or examples
- Address common objections
- Soft product mentions
- Educational CTA

### Value Email (Emails 5-7)
**Timing:** Days 8-14
**Goal:** Demonstrate expertise, show results
**Content:**
- Success stories
- Detailed how-to content
- Free tools or resources
- Product benefits (indirect)
- Engagement CTA

### Offer Email (Emails 8-9)
**Timing:** Days 15-20
**Goal:** Present offer, drive conversion
**Content:**
- Clear product/service presentation
- Benefits and features
- Social proof
- Special offer or bonus
- Strong CTA

### Close Email (Email 10)
**Timing:** Day 21-30
**Goal:** Final push, create urgency
**Content:**
- Last chance messaging
- Recap of benefits
- Scarcity or urgency
- Risk reversal
- Final CTA

## Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use numbers when relevant
- Create curiosity without clickbait
- Personalize with name or data
- Test emoji usage (industry-dependent)
- Avoid spam triggers (FREE, !!!, ALL CAPS)

### Email Copy
- Write in second person ("you")
- Conversational tone
- Short sentences and paragraphs
- One main idea per email
- Scannable formatting
- Mobile-optimized

### Design
- Single column layout
- Responsive design
- Alt text for images
- Text-to-image ratio 60:40
- Consistent branding
- White space

### CTAs
- One primary CTA
- Button vs. text link (test both)
- Contrasting colors
- Action verbs
- Benefit-focused
- Above and below fold

### Personalization
- Use first name
- Reference past behavior
- Segment-specific content
- Dynamic content blocks
- Location-based offers
- Purchase history

## Deliverability Best Practices

### Technical Setup
- SPF, DKIM, DMARC configured
- Dedicated IP (for high volume)
- Consistent sending domain
- Clean email list
- Double opt-in

### Content Optimization
- Avoid spam trigger words
- Balance text and images
- Include plain text version
- Unsubscribe link prominent
- Physical address included

### List Management
- Regular list cleaning
- Remove hard bounces immediately
- Suppress unsubscribes
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Segment inactive subscribers

### Sending Practices
- Consistent sending schedule
- Warm up new IPs
- Monitor engagement rates
- Respect frequency preferences
- Test before sending

## Metrics to Track

### Open Rate
- Industry average: 15-25%
- Influenced by subject line
- Time of send
- Sender name
- List quality

### Click-Through Rate
- Industry average: 2-5%
- Influenced by content relevance
- CTA placement
- Email design
- Audience segmentation

### Conversion Rate
- Industry average: 1-3%
- Influenced by offer quality
- Landing page experience
- Audience targeting
- Email sequence

### Unsubscribe Rate
- Healthy rate: <0.5%
- Monitor for spikes
- Indicates content/frequency issues
- Exit survey valuable

## Compliance Requirements

### CAN-SPAM Act (US)
- Accurate header information
- Non-deceptive subject lines
- Identify message as ad
- Include physical address
- Honor opt-out requests (10 days)

### GDPR (EU)
- Explicit consent required
- Easy unsubscribe process
- Data protection measures
- Right to be forgotten
- Privacy policy link

### CASL (Canada)
- Express or implied consent
- Identification information
- Unsubscribe mechanism
- Honor opt-outs promptly

## Email Sequence Examples

### SaaS Onboarding Sequence

**Email 1 - Welcome (Day 0)**
Subject: Welcome to [Product] - Let's Get Started
- Thank you for signing up
- Quick start guide
- First action to take
- Support resources

**Email 2 - Feature Highlight (Day 2)**
Subject: Discover [Key Feature]
- Introduce most valuable feature
- Use case example
- Video tutorial
- CTA to try feature

**Email 3 - Success Story (Day 5)**
Subject: How [Customer] Achieved [Result]
- Customer case study
- Specific results
- Similar use case
- CTA to book demo

**Email 4 - Tips & Tricks (Day 7)**
Subject: 5 Ways to Get More from [Product]
- Power user tips
- Hidden features
- Best practices
- CTA to upgrade

**Email 5 - Upgrade Offer (Day 14)**
Subject: Ready to Unlock Full Potential?
- Upgrade benefits
- Special offer
- Comparison chart
- CTA to upgrade

### E-commerce Abandoned Cart Sequence

**Email 1 - Reminder (1 hour)**
Subject: You Left Something Behind
- Cart contents
- Product images
- Easy checkout link
- Customer service offer

**Email 2 - Incentive (24 hours)**
Subject: Still Interested? Here's 10% Off
- Discount code
- Limited time offer
- Product benefits
- Social proof

**Email 3 - Final Chance (48 hours)**
Subject: Last Chance - Your Cart Expires Soon
- Urgency messaging
- Scarcity (low stock)
- Alternative products
- Final CTA
